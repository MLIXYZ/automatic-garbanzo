Our Planet's Climate Crisis

Understand the causes, impacts, and solutions to the most pressing environmental challenges of our time.

Learn the facts

Problem

Rising Temperatures

Human activities—primarily burning fossil fuels—have warmed the atmosphere, ocean, and land. The last few decades have been the warmest in modern records.

More Extreme Weather

Heatwaves, heavy rainfall, and droughts are becoming more frequent and intense, increasing risks to communities, infrastructure, and food systems.

Rising Seas

Melting land ice and warming oceans are driving sea‑level rise, threatening coastal cities with flooding, erosion, and saltwater intrusion.

Melting Ice

Glaciers and Arctic sea ice are shrinking, altering ecosystems and amplifying warming through loss of reflective ice surfaces.

Ocean Changes

The ocean absorbs most of the excess heat and a quarter of CO₂ emissions, causing warming and acidification that stress marine life and coral reefs.

Health Impacts

Climate change affects health via extreme heat, smoke and air pollution, changing disease patterns, food insecurity, and mental health stressors.

Solution

Cut Emissions

Rapidly scale clean electricity (wind, solar), electrify transport and buildings, improve efficiency, and phase down fossil fuels to reduce greenhouse gases.

Protect & Restore Nature

Conserve forests, wetlands, and soils that absorb carbon and protect biodiversity while providing clean water, cooler cities, and flood control.

Adapt & Build Resilience

Invest in cooling and shade, early‑warning systems, resilient infrastructure, and community preparedness—especially for the most vulnerable.
